Document Level Assessment of Information Retrieval Systems

Rsearch Internship under Dr. Sri Devi Ravana

This project was carried out during my internship which was for two months. The big task was to find out a better method to compare and evaluate Information Retrieval Systems. Significance Testing was done on the matched documents that existed between a pair of Information Retrieval Systems, to distinguish unique pairs of Information Retrieval Systems. The amount of significantly unique Information Retrieval Systems obtained from the proposed method were compared to the numbers obtained from the conventional methods to determine the efficiency of the proposed method. The data was obtained from TREC (Textual REtrieval Conference) website and R programming language was used to do analysis on the data. Based on the significantly better results yielded by the proposed method in comparison with the conventional method, a research paper was prepared and has been published in the Information Research Journal (ISSN 1368 - 1613), Vol. 22, No. 2, June 2017.
